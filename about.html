A little bit about me...

I grew up in the fabulous Florida Keys before moving to Naples, Florida in 2012. My hobbies include cooking (and eating, of course), outdoor adventures, and plenty of reading. Cats are also an unfortunate obsession of mine, and I try to foster kittens from the local humane society whenever I have time. For as long as I can remember, I have been captivated by the written word. Pursuing degrees in English and Journalism is a way for me to hopefully leave my own literary mark on the world.
